Overview

Purpose

The website will convey the times of year we run, sell the supplies people need and promote river running as a whole. The entire family needs to be advertised as having fun and that it is a safe activity for all no matter if they are fit or handicapped. One day and two day trips will be the most advertised as well as week long trips will be advertised for the more adventurous individuals.

The main message of our site will be: “Rafting is a fun activity for all to enjoy. Come and experience it for yourself and bring your family.”

Audience

  • Adventurous
  • Mid 20’s to 50
  • Upper middle class to upper class income
  • Healthy lifestyle
  • Family oriented for one and two day trips
  • Single professionals for week long trips

What do they want?

  • These people want a fun adventure to share with family or friends/co-workers
  • They want to experience something new to them
  • They want to re-experience the adrenaline rush you get while rafting.

Needs to being met?

  • Buy outdoor supplies relating to water activities
  • want a fun and safe way to satisfy an adrenalin rush

What devices will be use to access the site?

  • The site has to be desktop and mobile friendly. If it does not work on cell phones and tablets, Google will not give it high rankings in the SERP.

Heading Font: Halant

This is a nice wide and easy to read font for standing out as headings.

Paragraph Font: Nunito Sans

This is an easy to read font with lots of text around it. You can follow this text quite easily.

The best Whitewater Rafting in Colorado, White Water Rafting Company offers rafting on the Colorado and Roaring Fork Rivers in Glenwood Springs. Since 1974, we have been family owned and operated, rafting the Shoshone section of Glenwood Canyon and beyond.
