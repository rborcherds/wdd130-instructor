GitHub

DO NOT use any GitHub username with BYU, BYUI, Pathway, or PW or any combination of them in your GitHub username. This GitHub account is for you to use to showcase what you have learned as a developer, not just a student.

You can use your Pathway e-mail address, but I recommend using your personal e-mail address and you can add your byupathway.edu address as a secondary e-mail address on your account. You will only have the byupathway.edu e-mail address as long as you are a student.
