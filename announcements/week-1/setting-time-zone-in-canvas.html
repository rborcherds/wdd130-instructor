Setting the Time Zone in Canvas

One of the items you are supposed to do is to set your time zone in Canvas and upload a profile image. Setting your time zone I will say is mandatory.

 

Click on Account then Settings on the left navigation bar

 

Then click edit setting button on the right side

 

Click on the time zone option and select your time zone. This will adjust the due dates/time to be your local time in Canvas, so you know exactly when things are due.  Students in Africa need to look at the table below to know which setting in Canvas to use. Canvas does not list most countries in Africa.

 

 

Upload your profile image

Go to your Profile page and when you hover over the image area, you will see a pencil to edit/change the image

 

Rules for profile images.

  1. Must be of you. Do not use avatars, landscapes, pets, etc or images of your family
  2. It must be appropriate for a church/school. 

You will be able to edit the image a little and make it zoom in on your image.
