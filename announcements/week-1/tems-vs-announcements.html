Why use all three

The course discussion is occurring in Microsoft Teams. All help and questions about the course are in Microsoft Teams.

While Microsoft Teams will be the primary vehicle for course discussion and coordination, Canvas announcements and email have important roles as well.

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is the primary platform for all communication in this course. All course material help and additional materials are only posted in Microsoft Teams. There will be a lot of information that will only be posted in Microsoft Teams. You must check this multiple times a week.

Teams chat feature is for some private communication but most private communication must be done via email using the Canvas Inbox. If you ask questions for help in Chat, you will be asked to post the question in the Help Request Channel in Teams.

  • Login to teams with your Pathway email. You can access Teams using the Microsoft Education link in the left navigation in Canvas. 
  • Participation in the Microsoft Teams discussions are not optional and will be the primary communication method used in the course.
  • Download Microsoft Teams for your computer or mobile device or use the web interface of teams.
  • How to employ proper Teams etiquette.
  • Link to student instructions. Introduction to Microsoft Teams
  • First 3 posts in n Teams General Channel are about how to use teams in this class. Most of these items will apply to all classes.

Announcements in Canvas

Canvas Announcements is used for any official course business or announcements, such as changing the due dates or the scope of assignments.  There will be at least one announcement every week posted in Canvas Announcements. Week 1 has the most announcements.

Canvas Inbox (email)

E-mail should be used for any sensitive or personal communication. For example, a discussion about a health issue, or a missing assignment or a question about grades. Email is the official medium of communication recognized by BYU-Idaho and BYU Pathway Worldwide. You will get a lot of emails from the school.
