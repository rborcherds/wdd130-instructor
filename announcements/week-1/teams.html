There are 3 ways to access Teams. You have to log into Teams using your BYU Pathway email address. Let me know if you do not know what that is.

The first is to go to this URL and login with your BYU Pathway email address. https://teams.microsoft.com/v2/

The second way is to use the Microsoft Education link on the left in Canvas: 

Click the Sign In button

 

Use your BYU Pathway email address

Once you have signed in click the Teams button

That will launch the Teams web interface like the first option

 

The third and the most complex option to setup initially. This works with both the mobile app and desktop apps.

You must make sure that the Teams app you have installed is the most recent versions.

When you click the login option, you must use your BYU Pathway email address to login.

If you have issues with this let me  know and I can do a zoom meeting with you to get it setup.

If you are logging into Teams with multiple accounts, you will have multiple Teams icons running on your computer.

 

 

Once you are logged into Teams, you will see the teams you are a member of on the left. Click on the team for this class to see the channels and posts.
