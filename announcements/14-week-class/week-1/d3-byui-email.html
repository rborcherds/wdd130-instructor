It has come to my attention that a lot of you do not know that you do have an e-mail with @byui.edu. As someone that has attended classes through this university, you have this e-mail address for life.

Pathway Worldwide Students

 

From the Pathway dashboard, change the drop down in the center to say BYU-Idaho

 

Then click on your name in the top right corner.

There is a link to "Go to Email". Arrow in this image is pointing to the link

 

You will be given the normal BYU-Idaho or BYU Pathway Worldwide login page. Click the Pathway Worldwide button and login as normal.

This will take you to the Outlook online interface. If you have never accessed this e-mail account, you will have well 100 e-mails in there. This is especially true if you did not modify the notifications that Canvas sends on-demand by default.

 

It is very important that you check this e-mail address. Canvas sends notifications to this e-mail by default and almost every instructor sends e-mails using this e-mail address before the semester starts.

Once you know your BYU-Idaho e-mail address, you can login to your e-mail directly by going to: https://login.microsoftonline.com . You enter your BYU-Idaho e-mail address and then you will be prompted to login using the normal BYU-Idaho login page.

Pathway students find that from the instructions above. Other students find it on the my.byui.edu page under the Profile tab after you login.
