I am noticing that some of you are not using the colors from the site plan you created in week 2.

Week 4 - Colors must be used from the Site Plan. Do not make the Body background-color and the background color for any other rule the same. The body must be a different color from the rest of the background-colors. This means that if you change your colors in your site, you must update the site plan with those colors.

Week 5 - The fonts used in week 5 must be from the site plan. Any rule with the h1 to h6 must be the heading font from the site plan. All other rules will use the paragraph font.

The site plan is part of the grading when you submit the final in week 9 for the rafting site.

 

These rules will also apply to your personal site you will start to create in week 5.

  • Week 5 you record a video of your personal site proposal. I will give you feedback as to what aspects you are allowed to do in this class. This is where you start to take what you have learned and apply that knowledge into something you create. We do not give you code for the site, we do supply a new site plan template as there is a lot more info in this second site plan.

Week 7 to 9 you will create the site plan for your personal site.We give you the template to use and it is different than the one in week 2.

  • Week 7 you have to select the colors and fonts, write the purpose and audience.
  • Week 8 you have to create all the text and get at least 3 images per page. There are 3 pages required for the site. One will contain a form where users enter info. You do not capture that info in this class. 
  • Week 9 you create the wireframes for all 3 pages you will create.
  • Week 10 you write the HTML code for all 3 pages on your own. No CSS yet. This is graded according to the content in the site plan you wrote and the images used from week 8.
  • Week 11 to 13 you style each page and submit for grading according to your wireframes and site plan info, fonts, colors, etc.
  • Week 14 you submit a final report and the URL to your final site. This is again graded against the site plan.

 

 

You will notice that Week 6 is not mentioned above. This is because you will be learning about grid layout which is a requirement in the rafting site and your personal site. Week 7 has you apply the grid layout and some flexbox layout to the homepage of the rafting site.
