The <div id="background"></div> background color must:

  • be a different color from the body
  • cannot be the same color as the <section class="msg"> background color

You must change the text color for the items in step 13.

  • nav a
  • nav a:hover
  • .home-title
  • h4
  • .book, .join
  • .book:hover, .join:hover
  • .msg h2
  • msg p
  • footer
  • footer a
  • footer a:hover

The background colors mentioned above are for the image with the text over it and to the right at the bottom. You can also see why the text color must be changed as this image shows all the text colors that the instructions tell you to change.

 

The footer is a standard background color used on a lot of sites. The instructions do not specifically tell you to change the footer background but I highly recommend it.

If you have a dark color scheme then make the footer a lighter color. If you color scheme is lighter then do a dark footer. You want the footer to denote the end of the page by being in complete contrast with the rest of the page. Mu color is #3e3e3e for the footer background color.
