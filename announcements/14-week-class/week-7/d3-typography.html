This video will tell you to download things from the Envato+ website. Keep in mind that they are trying to sell you things and you do not need to download what they ask you to download. They do have good resources but most of them do cost money. I only share things that you can do for free.

 

There are a lot of resources on YouTube to show you how to use fonts. Be careful with videos that are specific to a product. There are quite a few that are about using typography in WordPress themes for example. Do not watch those. There are also a lot around products like Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, etc. They may be useful but you need to understand how to apply to your application.
