Colors and Fonts

I wish to share a couple of useful sites at this time.  These may help you with selecting colors and fonts for your personal site. This post is long but most is links to other sites. One of the reasons I give so many links is that different writing styles will resonate with different students. You find the style that you understand and read that full article.  If it does not get your attention quickly move onto another article.

As you can see, there are a lot of ways to get to the same conclusion, but every person learns how to get to that end a different way and it is explained in different ways because we all think differently. Use this as a guide to help you find the process that makes the most sense to you.
