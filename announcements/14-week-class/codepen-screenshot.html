Getting a screenshot from Codepen

The Poem Alignment assignment requires you to use codepen.io to do the assignment. Do not use your local machine and publish to GitHub.

There are two ways to get to a full screen capture of the poem in the alignment boxes.

 

The first way

In the bottom there is a button that says FORK. Click on that button. This will make a copy of the project into a personal work area that you can save it and look at it in full screen mode.

There is a second way.

 

This require the installation of an extension called Capture & Print. This will allow you to capture a page and even scroll down the page.

Click on the extension icon and then select what you want to capture.

This will make a graphic image of that in a new tab. (You must allow pop-ups for this to work)

You can then right click and select Save Image As.

 

Extension for Firefox that works well:   https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/capture-print/

 

Extension for Chrome, Edge, and other Chromium browsers. This is just one of many that will work:

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/screen-capture/pmnphobdokkajkpbkajlaiooipfcpgio
