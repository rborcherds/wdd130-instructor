Smartphones do not have those fonts either. It is always better to use Google Fonts or similar services for web development. In the workplace you may host your own fonts on your server.

Starting on your Personal Website Idea

This week you will have to create an elevator pitch for your personal website . You will submit the pitch about the website idea. Start early on this so that you will have the time to complete it correctly.

This is the way the pitch needs to be written and must be 75 to 100 words. What would you say in 60 seconds on the idea.

  1. Start with the idea of "I am going to create a website that does/shows/teaches/ etc"  Do not do a pitch for a company as a whole. This is about your website only.
  2. To come up with the idea think about it like these 2 examples stating questions.
    1. You are with a few friends and you want to teach them something you enjoy. What is that idea and how would you go about putting that onto a web site.
    2. You have a hobby you enjoy. What is it and how would you explain it to other people.

The idea cannot be an e-commerce, research gathering, something that will require a database, blog type site or forums/discussion type site.

Let me know if you have any questions on this topic.
