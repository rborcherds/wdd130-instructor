As you are starting to work on your personal site site-plan, I wanted to share with you an example of a personal site and finished site-plan.

 

Here is the pitch/purpose for the site.

"Create a website that showcases the pioneer trek from April 2022. This trek was attended by the stakes of Yucca Valley and Victorville. This site will explain what is a pioneer trek, what types of activities where done on the trek and what inspirational stories were told to the youth. This site will be more of an overview of what occurred. It will also have a couple of videos, a bunch of images of the youth dressed in pioneer era clothing, pulling handcarts, and ding the other activities."

 

Here is a link to my GitHub pages site so you can look at the site and the site-plan. You will be doing something very similar.

I did make a few modifications to the site-plan to accomplish what I wanted. I added a 3rd accent colors and changed the alignment. I also change the default sizes for the heading tags.

 

I kept the wireframes to the most basic of layouts to illustrate how basic they can be and still be valid for this class as an example. You will notice that no real text was use and not images were used in the wireframe. The wireframe is supposed to depict the general layout of the page. You will create wireframes during weeks 8 & 9.
