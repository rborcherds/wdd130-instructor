Here are links to personal sites that students have created in past semesters. Most of these URL's will take you to a site called Glitch. We used to use that platform for this class until Spring 2022 semester for the full semester students and this semester Fall 2022 for the block students.

Each of these sites have something that is good about them. I am not going to tell you what or why for most of the sites. There is one I do want to point out. Things like the images, the layout, the colors and/or fonts used, the topic of the site are all reasons I like these examples. This is less than 20% of the sites that students have created while I have been teaching.

 

Each example site lists the URL, the title of the site and then the type of content, if needed, so you can get ideas quickly for your site.

 

The first site was created by a student that is visually impaired. If he can do this, you can learn to create a website and finish this class.  He also did this class as a block class, meaning he did all the work in 7 weeks.
