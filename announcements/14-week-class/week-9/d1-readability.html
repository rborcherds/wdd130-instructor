Last week I posted some helps about writing content for your site.

One of the items was to keep the content simple and easy to understand. TO help with with here is a good site to use.

https://www.webfx.com/tools/read-able/

You enter the URL and click the green button. It will then take a look at all the words and sentences on your page and calculate a variety of reading scores.

 

Just something fun to see how you write. There are other such types of standards in almost all languages. You will just need to find the ones that are considered the standard for your language.

 

Here are a few others that you have to paste the text into the site to calculate the score.

https://goodcalculators.com/flesch-kincaid-calculator/

https://www.textcompare.org/readability/flesch-kincaid-grade-level/
