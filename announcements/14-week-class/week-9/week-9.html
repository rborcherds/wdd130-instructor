What to expect.

The first activity you will do is an ICE Challenge.

 

There are 2 big deadlines this week.

The first is the completion of the Dry Oar website (the one we have been working on from the beginning of the semester.)

 

The second big deadline is the finished site plan. This is all the text for all 3 pages including images and the wireframes for your personal site. The instructions state

  1. Significant textual content consisting of appropriate content for your audience so that they can understand the meaning and message of this site

This means at least 300 words for each page. You will not use that many on the page but the more content you have the easier it will be to create the text for the web page.

There are 2 parts to the Site Plan document activity. Make sure you go through both parts as they will help you gather your thoughts and give you ideas on where to get the images, etc. Always remember to use the Principles of Good Design ( https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a5KYlHNKQB8&feature=emb_imp_woyt )

 

You will create 3 wireframes for the pages you will create. You are not doing any HTML coding for your personal site yet. You do not need to put real text in the wireframe. Just put blocks denoting text areas or use Lorem Ipsum text.

 

 

Quite a few of you have had issues exporting the wireframes as images.
