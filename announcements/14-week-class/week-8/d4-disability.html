Disability laws

There is a law that is not taught in any class specifically for web development, but one you must be aware that it does exists. Most new developers do not even think about this issue.

Your website must be usable by people that have color blindness or even are totally blind. I want to address the color blindness in this post. The blind users issue could be a 3 to 5 week module just on that.

I posted last week a list of link to various sites that allow you to create color schemes. One of these is https://paletton.com/

Links to an external site. . If you choose your colors with this site, it has a Visual Simulation that will show you what people with various color blindness will see when looking at your site.

One of the ways to address this is to also make sure that your site has good color contrast.  Read this article for a lot more info on this and why it is important https://webaim.org/articles/contrast/
