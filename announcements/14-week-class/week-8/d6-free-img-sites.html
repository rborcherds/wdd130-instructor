Here is a list of free image sites that I have used in the past. Keep in mind that some of these sites have a lot of ads and some have free and paid images. This list is in alphabetical order. I do not suggest one over the other.

Also keep in mind that quite a few of these sites do have images that are not approved for use in school/church related content. This is especially true for paid image sites.

 

The best images to use are images that you create yourself. Remember to always optimize the image or download images that are less than 1mb in size if possible for use in your sites.
