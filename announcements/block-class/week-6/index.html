What you will be doing for Week 6 & 7 in the same week

There is nothing to submit technically for week 6. You do have some learning activities

You will study the Web Developer Roadmap and the Web Designer Roadmap and learn what it takes to do both jobs. You will also learn about interview questions for the Web Designer

The font-end roadmap has a lot of information on it. This is very subjective and personal choice. It does give a great overview of all the optional languages, skills etc that you will need to learn to be successful as a web font-end developer.

The Web Designer has a section about building your portfolio to showcase your work.

You will do some research on potential jobs that you will be able to do including how to find an internship and post about it in the General Channel in Teams.

 

The rest of the week will be spent on building the last 2 pages of your website. You will build the trips page and the home page.

Remember that the entire 4-page website due by June 1, with late submissions by June 6. The goal for the graders is to have everything graded by June 10, so that I will have time to review the final grades and get them submitted by 12:00 PM, June 12. MDT. The grades become official on June 13, when you will have access to them.

The second block starts on June 10. If you do not have any class scheduled for then and would like to take more classes, you must go through the portal and request to register for the classes in the second block.
