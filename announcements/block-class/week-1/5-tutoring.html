There are free tutoring resources available in this class! 

Tutors are students or former students who have experience in this course. They are excited to help you do well! Tutoring will help you succeed. There are two types of tutor offerings:

Group Study Sessions are led by a tutor and all students are welcome. Come with questions on this week’s assignments or just come to learn from other students’ questions. To see the upcoming group study sessions or sign up to receive reminders, please visit https://bit.ly/3TVS3xX.

Personal Study Sessions with a tutor are helpful if you are finding the course or a topic difficult. The tutor can meet with you personally for a 30 minute session. You need to schedule Personal Study Sessions at least 12 hours in advance. To schedule a Personal Study Session with a tutor, please visit https://bit.ly/4aD2L25.

 

Keep in mind that asking for help is not a sign of weakness but one of strength and it is one of the fastest ways to learn new things. It is also a great habit to get into for when you are working with a team. You all have to learn something, and no-one has all the answers. It helps you get the work done faster.

All online tutoring resources are free and open to all students in this course!
