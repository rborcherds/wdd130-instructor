What you will be doing for Week 6.

The Web Designer has a section about building your portfolio to showcase your work.

You will do some research on potential jobs that you will be able to do including how to find an internship and post about it in the Week 06 Forum Channel in Teams.

The rest of week 6 will be spent on building the last 2 pages of your website. You will build the trips page and the home page. Make sure to go back and fix issues from weeks 3 to 5 as things such as the title, meta description, color contrast, etc. will be verified on all pages. HTML and CSS errors must be fixed for all pages as well. Those will affect your Week 6 grade.

One very important piece of knowledge for week 6. Submit before the end of the week so you have a chance to resubmit for a higher grade. If you submit on Saturday and into week 7, there is a very high chance that you will not get the opportunity to resubmit as the class will lock on Thursday, October 16 at 11:59 PM UTC.
